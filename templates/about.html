About the Project

Bridging the communication gap with Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission

The Indian Sign Language Detection (ISLD) project aims to empower the deaf and hard-of-hearing community by providing a seamless real-time translation system. We leverage advanced Machine Learning (LSTM models) and Computer Vision to convert complex ISL gestures into spoken language and vice versa.

Core Features

Real-time Detection

Uses MediaPipe for precise landmark tracking and LSTM for temporal gesture recognition.

Speech Synthesis

Converts recognized signs into clear, audible speech instantly.

Inclusive Design

Built with a focus on accessibility and ease of use for everyone.

How it Works

When a user performs a sign in front of the camera, the system extracts 150 feature points representing pose and hand landmarks. These points are processed as a temporal sequence (30 frames) by our custom-trained Neural Network, which identifies the sign with high precision.
